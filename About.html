I am currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science, and I have developed a strong passion for using technology to solve real-world problems. Throughout my studies, I’ve become particularly interested in Full Stack Development, as it allows me to work on both the front-end and back-end of web applications.

I enjoy creating intuitive user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while also building robust back-end systems with technologies like Node.js and MongoDB. The ability to understand how all layers of an application work together excites me, and I’m always eager to learn new tools and frameworks to improve my skills. As I continue my journey, I aim to contribute to building innovative and user-friendly tech solutions that make a positive impact.

Education

My journey in education began at FIITJEE, where I spent my school years up until 10th grade. FIITJEE provided me with a rigorous academic environment, focusing not only on school subjects but also on preparing me for competitive exams. This foundation helped me build strong problem-solving skills and instilled in me a disciplined approach to my studies, laying the groundwork for my future academic pursuits.

In 11th and 12th grade, I transitioned to Modulus where I continued my preparation for competitive exams while deepening my understanding of core subjects. The environment at Modulus was more specialized, and I was able to focus more intently on subjects that would be crucial for my entrance exams. The challenging curriculum at Modulus further sharpened my academic abilities and gave me a clearer direction toward my goal of pursuing a career in technology.

Currently, I am pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri, as part of the 2024 batch. The transition to university has been an exciting chapter, filled with new opportunities to explore my interests in technology, particularly in Full Stack Development. Amrita has provided me with an excellent platform to develop both my technical skills and soft skills, and I look forward to the experiences and challenges ahead as I continue my journey toward becoming a proficient software developer.
